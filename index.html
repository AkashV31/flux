The Problem

Traditional finance apps are built for salaries. You do not have one.

Blind budgeting
Standard apps tell you what you spent yesterday. They cannot tell you if the next seven days are strong enough to carry the month.
Feast and famine
When your income swings between high-output weeks and quiet ones, static monthly budgeting becomes exhausting very quickly.
Guilt-driven burnout
Without a clear safety picture, even a short break can feel irresponsible. That uncertainty keeps people working past the point of clarity.
The Solution

A system designed to absorb volatility.

Income Forecasting
Flux anticipates high-earning days, low-pressure windows, and likely swings before they turn into surprises.
The Safety Vault
Automated rules stash away surplus cash on your strongest days so slow weeks feel manageable instead of threatening.
Break Planner
Model the exact cost of time off, compare start windows, and see whether the break is actually safe before you commit.
